The Island of Cozumel

Cozumel is the largest inhabited island in Mexico and has some of the most beautiful beaches in the world. There is a plethora of activites available that are fun for the whole family. With a rental vehicle from Scoots, you can reach every part of the island.

About Us

Hello and welcome to Scoots Rental and Tours! Scoots is the largest distributor of rental vehicles on the island of Cozumel. We offer a wide selection of scooters and 4x4s for your transportation needs while staying on the island. Everything we offer is kept in pristine condition and working order. We have two convenient locations just around the corner from everything you'll need.
