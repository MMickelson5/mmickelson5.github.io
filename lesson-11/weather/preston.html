Is your family safe?

It's monsoon season again in our beautiful town of Preston. Monsoon season is a wonderful for our agriculture and lawns, but with monsoons come dangers like lightning, flash flooding, and oppressive winds. How do you keep your family safe?

First, ensure those dependent on you know safe water interaction. 1) Never drive a vehicle in standing water where you can't see the bottom clearly. 2) Always travel with a companion when weather gets nasty. 3) Keep a reserve of food and water ready in case of long term power outages.
