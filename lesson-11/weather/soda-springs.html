As The Chill Sets In

It's that time of season again. A time to gather together with family, but also a time to scrap that pesky frost off your windshields. It seems to most that father winter has greeted us with copius amounts of snow and cold wind to boot. Although it seems like a burden, experts say the early snow is an indication of shifting weather patterns in our area. This could mean extra moisture for the crops our town will desperately need in the coming years and many more to come

So, next time you want to gripe because of the extra time you need to scrap your car clear of ice, think of your local farmers and be glad that they can continue their businesses
